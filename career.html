Past


During my time in high school at Sacred Heart Cathedral Prep, I played on the school volleyball team up to varsity. We would fundraise for our team by grilling hamburgers and hot dogs during basketball or women's volleyball games. Also in high school, I volunteered to work the voting polls for the 2010 San Francisco local elections.
During my time in undergraduate at UCSB, I was involved in Pi Alpha Phi, an asian-american interest fraternity. As a part-time job, I worked for ASPB(Associated Students Program Board) as part of the production crew there. We helped to set-up and organize shows/concerts for the student body. On the side, I volunteered for KCSB FM 91.9 as part of the Sports Crew. I would help to broadcast any athletic games ranging from basketball, soccer, volleyball, or baseball(unfortunatley, we didn't have a football team at UCSB). On some days, I would try and sit in with the math lectures going on at SIAM(Society of Industrial and Applied Mathematics).

Current


I'm currently enrolled in Galvanize's Web Development Immersive program. On the side, I volunteer as part of the core-team and Research group at Code for San Francisco. In addition, I also volunteer with AAMA(Asian-American Multitechnology Association) to help set-up and coordinate their events.

To infinity and beyond!


It was always a dream of mine to explore uncharted territories, whether it be the deepest depths of the ocean or the sheer vastness of outer space. I've always been fascinated with how technology works ever since I received my first GameBoy all the way to building my very own desktop computer. I'm beginning to learn the intricacies of software engineering. I'm also discovering a hidden passion for architecture and interior design.
After Galvanize, I would love to work for a company like SpaceX, Blue Origins, AirBnB, Twitch, or Blizzard Entertainment.
