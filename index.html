About me


Hi, my name is Kevin Lam. I'm just your terribly average Asian-American who happens to enjoy terribly average things. My parents immigrated to this country from Vietnam to escape the violence of the Vietnam War. I was born and raised in the lovely city of San Francisco. I attained a Bachelor of Arts degree in Mathematics and a minor in Black Studies from the University of California, Santa Barbara.

Some of my hobbies include playing video games, reading about philosophy or any stem-related topics, playing a good game of full court basketball, trying out a new recipe, architecture hunting, indulging in either a pint of 21st Amendment IPA or a nice bowl of Blue Dream(Thank you Prop 64) while listening to some low-fidelity hip hop after a long day, or just stretching and meditating my worries away.
You're probably thinking this guy is extremely basic ^

Aaaand that's my life and what I like to do with my free time. I hope I didn't bore you with my incredibly generic choice of words.
